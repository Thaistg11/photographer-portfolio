ABOUT ME

Hello, I am Luigi Rossi. A weddings and lifestyle photographer based in Richmond, Virginia.

As a lifestyle photographer, I am a creative professional who specializes in capturing candid, authentic, and emotionally resonant moments in people's lives. I am passionate about documenting real-life situations and emotions, rather than posed or staged shots. I work in various settings, including outdoor locations, homes, events, and public spaces, to capture the everyday moments, events, and experiences of my subjects.
